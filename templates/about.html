ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This website demonstrates the prototype focused on sustainability in digital media. The goal is to provide an accessible platform for video compression that reduces file size and energy consumption without compromising too much on quality.

Users can upload short videos and compress them using sustainable algorithms like H.264 and H.265, with energy usage and compression stats presented after each task. The platform is designed to support efficient testing, sharing, and demonstration of eco-friendly compression techniques.

It is built with a user-friendly interface and uses a fixed power draw calculation to estimate energy consumption, demonstrating how green computing can be practical, impactful, and accessible.
